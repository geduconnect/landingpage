Online BBA

Programme Description

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program is an undergraduate degree offering a comprehensive understanding of business essentials. Core courses in BBA cover business principles, management skills, and quantitative analysis. Emphasis on communication and ethics in the program enhances students' professional skills. The program integrates a global perspective, focusing on international business concepts. Technology-related courses ensure familiarity with modern business tools. Soft skills development, including teamwork and critical thinking, is a key focus in BBA. BBA graduates are well-prepared for entry-level business positions or further education, such as specialized master's programs or an MBA.

Eligibility

Entry Level Qualifications for BBA Programme are as per the following: A pass in 12th Class from any Recognized Board.

Course Duration

3 Years

Program Objectives

  • Acquire the skills required to manage and control business functions effectively.
  • Demonstrate planning decisions in an organization with the various methods of planning.
  • Develop soft skills such as communication, leadership, teamwork, and more.
  • Learn about financial accounting, business law, statistics, operations management, etc.
  • Gain the necessary knowledge and skills to understand the fundamental principles of business.
